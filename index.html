Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom home build take in the Bay Area?
Most custom home projects take 12–18 months from permit approval to move-in. Our Design-Build process significantly reduces timeline by combining design and construction phases under one roof.
Do you handle permits and city approvals?
Yes — permit acquisition is fully included in our service. We're deeply familiar with Bay Area requirements, including Palo Alto's rigorous review process and Saratoga's hillside development regulations. You don't lift a finger on paperwork.
What's the difference between Design-Build and hiring separately?
With Design-Build, one team manages both design and construction — eliminating miscommunication, reducing costs, and speeding up timelines. Separate architect + contractor often means change orders, delays, and blame-shifting. We built our model to fix that.
What is your typical project budget range?
Custom home construction in the Bay Area typically ranges from $400–$800 per square foot depending on finishes and complexity. We provide free consultations and transparent, detailed estimates before any work begins — no surprises.
Are you licensed and insured in California?
Yes. Square Builders Group holds a current California Contractor's License and carries full general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We're happy to provide documentation upon request.
